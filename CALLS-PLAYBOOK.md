# 📞 2+ Calls Every Day From Google — The Playbook

Goal: **minimum 2 calls/day (60+/month) from Google Search + Maps** for
Zaid Electronics. This file is the operating manual — what to do, in what
order, with copy-paste assets. Based on 2026 local-SEO data (sources at bottom).

---

## 0. The honest math first

Where Google calls come from, per 2026 data:

- Businesses in the **local 3-pack capture ~44% of all local clicks**.
- **15–24% of Google Business Profile (GBP) actions are phone calls**, and
  mobile users are **2.3× more likely to call** — and your customers are
  almost all on mobile.
- So: **~60 calls/month needs roughly 280–400 GBP actions/month, which needs
  roughly 4,000–6,000 profile views/month.** In a catchment as dense as
  Trombay–Chembur–Govandi–Mankhurd, a top-3 profile for "tv repair near me"
  type searches reaches that comfortably.

**Timeline honesty:** ranking movement takes **60–90 days** of consistent
work. Organic alone won't guarantee 2 calls/day in week 1. The plan below
runs two tracks:

- **Track A (free, compounds):** GBP + reviews + website → owns the goal
  permanently from month ~2–3.
- **Track B (paid, instant):** small Google Ads budget → guarantees the
  2 calls/day from day 1, then taper it off as Track A takes over.

**STEP ZERO — get the baseline.** Open Google Business Profile → Performance.
Note current monthly **calls, direction requests, website clicks, views**.
Everything below is measured against this number weekly.

## 1. Track A — the #1 lever: review velocity (do this before anything else)

2026's biggest finding: **review recency is now the #1 individual local
ranking factor.** A shop getting steady fresh reviews outranks one sitting on
old ones. You have 4.9★ × 190 — a great base — but Google now rewards *flow*,
not stock.

**The system (non-negotiable):**
1. After EVERY completed repair, send this WhatsApp within 1 hour, while the
   customer is happy. Target: **2 asks/day → 4–8 new reviews/month minimum.**
2. Reply to every review within 24–48h (response rate is a ranking signal).

**Copy-paste review ask (English):**
> Thank you for trusting Zaid Electronics with your TV! 🙏 If you're happy
> with the repair, a 1-minute Google review helps our small shop a lot:
> [YOUR REVIEW LINK]
> It helps if you mention what we fixed and your area (e.g. "fixed my Samsung
> panel lines, Chembur").

**Hindi:**
> Zaid Electronics पर भरोसा करने के लिए धन्यवाद! 🙏 अगर आप रिपेयर से खुश हैं,
> तो 1 मिनट का Google review हमारी छोटी दुकान की बहुत मदद करेगा:
> [YOUR REVIEW LINK]
> हो सके तो लिखें क्या ठीक हुआ और आपका एरिया (जैसे "TV पैनल ठीक किया, गोवंडी")।

**Marathi:**
> Zaid Electronics वर विश्वास ठेवल्याबद्दल धन्यवाद! 🙏 रिपेअरने समाधान झाले
> असेल तर 1 मिनिटाचा Google review आमच्या छोट्या दुकानाला खूप मदत करेल:
> [YOUR REVIEW LINK]

Get YOUR REVIEW LINK: GBP dashboard → "Ask for reviews" → copy the short
link. Print it as a QR sticker on the counter + on every warranty slip.

**Why area+service mentions matter:** reviews containing "panel repair" and
"Govandi" literally rank you for those words.

**Review reply templates:**
- Positive: "Thank you [name]! Enjoy the TV — remember your repair carries a
  90-day written warranty. — Asif, Zaid Electronics"
- Negative: "Sorry your experience wasn't perfect, [name]. Please call me
  directly on 98214 73182 — I'll personally sort it out. — Asif" (never argue;
  future customers read the reply, not the complaint.)

## 4. Track B — guaranteed calls from day 1 (Google Ads)

While Track A ramps (60–90 days), a small ads budget guarantees the target.
2026 India benchmarks for local repair services: **CPC ₹10–60, cost per
lead ₹150–400.**

- **Budget: ₹500/day (~₹15,000/month)** → realistically **1.5–3 calls/day**
  on top of current organic. Scale down after month 2 as organic grows.
- ⚠️ **Call-only ads were killed in Feb 2026.** The correct setup now:
  **Responsive Search Ads + call assets + location assets**, campaign goal
  "Phone calls".
- Settings that matter:
  - Location: 6–8 km radius around Cheeta Camp (Trombay, Chembur, Govandi,
    Mankhurd, Kurla, Sion, Wadala, Ghatkopar) — "presence" targeting only.
  - Ad schedule: 10:00–21:00 (only when you can answer!). Missed call = burned money.
  - Keywords (exact/phrase): tv repair near me, tv repair [area], led tv
    repair, lcd tv repair, tv screen repair, tv panel repair, टीवी रिपेयर.
  - Negative keywords: job, course, training, parts wholesale, remote (as in
    TV remote sales), second hand mobile.
  - Landing pages: send area keywords to the matching /tv-repair-[area]/ page.
- Track in Google Ads "calls" reporting + compare GBP baseline.

**Unit economics sanity check:** at ₹250/lead and your average repair ticket
(₹1,500–4,000), every ad rupee returns 6–15×. This is the cheapest customer
acquisition you will ever buy — until organic makes it free.

## 5. The weekly scorecard (5 min, every Monday)

| Metric | Where | Target trend |
|---|---|---|
| Calls from GBP | GBP → Performance → Calls | +10%/month → 45+/mo |
| Calls from Ads | Google Ads → Calls | fills the gap to 60/mo |
| New reviews this week | GBP | ≥1–2/week |
| Profile views | GBP Performance | growing |
| Search queries showing you | GBP Performance → queries | more areas appearing |
| Site clicks/impressions | Search Console | landing pages gaining |

When **GBP calls alone ≥ 60/month** (typically month 3–4 if the review system
runs every day), cut the ads budget to ₹200/day or zero. That's the win state:
2+ calls/day, free, compounding.
