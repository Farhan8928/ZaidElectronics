[ 06 / FAQ ]Questions

Asked at the counter, daily

Something else on your mind? Ask on WhatsApp — we reply within minutes during shop hours.

  1. Q.1

    How much does TV repair cost in Mumbai?

    It depends on the fault: power/backlight issues are the most affordable, panel bonding costs roughly a third of panel replacement. Diagnosis is free — you get an exact fixed quote on WhatsApp before any work starts.

  2. Q.2

    Is the diagnosis really free?

    Yes. Whether you bring the set to the shop or we visit your home, checking the fault costs nothing. You pay only if you approve the repair quote.

  3. Q.3

    My screen has lines. Do I need a new panel?

    Usually not. Lines are most often a COF/TAB bonding fault — repairable on our bonding machine for up to 70% less than panel replacement. We tell you honestly if a panel is truly beyond repair.

  4. Q.4

    How fast can you repair my TV?

    Power, backlight and software faults are usually quick to fix. Panel bonding takes 1–3 days. You get a time estimate along with your quote before any work starts.

  5. Q.5

    Do you repair at home?

    Yes — doorstep visits across Mumbai, 7 days a week. Wall-mounted and big-screen sets are repaired at home where possible; otherwise pickup and drop is free.

  6. Q.6

    Which brands do you repair?

    All major brands — Samsung, LG, Sony, Mi, OnePlus, TCL, Panasonic, Videocon, Onida, BPL and more. LED, LCD, Smart, Android, 4K and QLED.
